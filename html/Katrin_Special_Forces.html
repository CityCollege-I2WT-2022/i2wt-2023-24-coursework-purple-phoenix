Bulgarian Special Forces

Bulgarian Special Forces

The Special Forces Brigade is a key component of the Bulgarian Armed Forces. It is trained and equipped to carry out a variety of special operations, including reconnaissance, counter-terrorism, and unconventional warfare.

Role and Responsibility

Bulgarian Special Forces are trained to handle a wide range of tasks, including intelligence gathering, direct action, special reconnaissance, and counterterrorism operations. They may also participate in international missions as part of Bulgaria's commitments to NATO or other security alliances.

Training and Professionalism

Special Forces personnel undergo rigorous training to develop the skills required for their specialized roles. Training programs often include elements of physical fitness, marksmanship, tactical skills, and mission-specific training.

National Service for Counterterrorism

The National Service for Counterterrorism is responsible for addressing counterterrorism activities within Bulgaria. It works in coordination with other law enforcement and security agencies to respond to potential terrorist threats.
