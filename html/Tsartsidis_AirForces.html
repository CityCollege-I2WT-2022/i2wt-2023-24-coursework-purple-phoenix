Greek Air Force

Meet the secretive Greek military unit that NATO Special Operations HQ just rated as 'combat ready' and 'exceptional'

In the closing days of 2021, a little-known Greek special-operations unit received NATO's highest readiness qualification. Evaluators from the NATO Special Operations Forces Headquarters (NSHQ) traveled to Greece and put the country's elite special operators through the wringer. The Special Paratroopers Section, the tier-one special-operations unit of the Hellenic Army, not only passed with top marks but also achieved the highest possible rating.
