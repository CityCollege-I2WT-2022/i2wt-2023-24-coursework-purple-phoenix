How to get in

There are several ways to get into the Spanish army:

  • >As an officer

    • For joining the army as an Official:

    • With no titulation
    • With titulation

    With no titulation


    For join with out the titulation you need to pass present the overage of highschool and the overage of the EBAU Exams; the mark is calculated this way:
    O = B*0.6 + L*0.1 + E*0.1 + H*0.1 + M*0.3 + P*0.2
    O = overage over 14.
    B = Overage of highschool.
    L = EBAU exam of literature.
    E = EBAU exam of english.
    H = EBAU exam of history.
    M = EBAU exam of maths.
    P = EBAU exam of Physics.
    When you have your overage mark you present yourself to the first phase of the contest in wich all spain can take part of, so the overage mark for passing this phase is changing every year but on 2023 was a 12 over 14.
    The second phase consist of some physical fitness test where they are going to test your physical aptitud, psychological test, and medical test.
    If you pass all those test you go to prepare yourself for 5 years in Zaragoza.

    • Enter with EBAU

    • 1º course AGM
    • 2º course AGM
    • 3º course AGM
    • 4º course AGM
    • 5º AC ESPFUN course
    • Lieutenant+ Carrear diplome

    With titulation

    For join the army with titulation you need to have a degree in something related with the science and then with the mark you pass to the contest part in wich you are going to be calificated following the next formula:
    CF = PI + PFC
    CF = Final mark.
    PI = English test. that you need to take.
    PFC = Mark of the degree.
    Then if you are selected after all the proces you go the the AGM for 3 years.

    How to get in

    First you need to choose the specialty, then you need to pass the selective phase, and then the final mark is based in this formula:
    PFC = NMB + FG + VMM
    PFC = Final grade.
    NNMB = Overage grade of highschool.
    FG = EBAU exams.
