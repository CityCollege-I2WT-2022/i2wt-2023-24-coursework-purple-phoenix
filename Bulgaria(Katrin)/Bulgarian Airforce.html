Bulgarian Airforce

History

Throughout its history, the Bulgarian Armed Forces have adapted to changing geopolitical circumstances, and the country has played roles in various military alliances and conflicts.

Modernization

Bulgaria has been actively engaged in modernizing its armed forces to enhance capabilities, meet NATO standards, and address contemporary security challenges.

Aircraft

Bulgarian military aviation primarily consists of aircraft acquired from other countries, and it has been a user of Soviet and Russian-made aircraft.

NATO Membership

Bulgaria joined NATO in 2004, becoming a member of the alliance. This marked a significant step in aligning Bulgarian defense policies and capabilities with those of NATO.

Role

The Bulgarian Air Force plays a critical role in the defense and security of Bulgaria. Its primary mission is to protect the sovereignty of Bulgarian airspace, contribute to NATO collective defense efforts, and support national and international security objectives.

Training and Collaboration

Training and collaboration are crucial aspects of the Bulgarian Air Force's efforts to maintain readiness, enhance capabilities, and foster cooperation with other military forces, particularly within the NATO framework.
