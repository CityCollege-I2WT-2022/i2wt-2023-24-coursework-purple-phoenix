Equipment The Romanian Air Force operates various aircraft, including F-16 Fighting Falcons, C-27J Spartan transport planes, and IAR 330 Puma helicopters.

International Operations The Romanian Air Force has been involved in international operations, contributing to missions such as air policing in the Baltic region and participating in NATO and UN peacekeeping efforts.

Training and exercises The Romanian Air Force conducts regular training and exercises to enhance operational readiness, airspace protection, and interoperability with allied forces.

Modernization Efforts The Romanian Air Force is actively modernizing its capabilities through the acquisition of advanced aircraft, such as F-16s, and the ongoing upgrade of its fleet to ensure enhanced operational effectiveness.
